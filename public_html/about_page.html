About Us





WELCOME!

Learn a little more about us!

We are an Indian restaurant located at 200 Bishnu Ave in West Des Moines, Iowa 50159. New Delhi Restaurant was founded by Bishnu Adhikari and Brett Dewerff in 1992. We have been published in Brett's Newspaper four times since 1992 as the best Indian restaurant in Iowa. We are proud to serve everyone and hope that all of our customers will leave satisfied after every visit. Our priority is providing a positive experience for our visitors including selections of our authentic Indian cuisine, polite restaurant staff, and clean dining rooms and facilities.



We plan to expand to different parts of Iowa while maintaining outstanding service and award-winning entrees. We also plan to open a bar at the West Des Moines location in the near future, so we can expand our hours of operation and offer other means of entertainment to our valued customers. Our restaurant is a weekend hotspot in West Des Moines as it is located in a great area for a night on the town after an amazing meal. We pride ourselves on offering service with a smile. If there's anything we can do to make your dining experience more enjoyable, just ask! We are all friends here at New Delhi Restaurant.
